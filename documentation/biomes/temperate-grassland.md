# TemperateGrassland Biome Prefabs

## Direction
Open meadows with mixed oak growth, bright low shrub coverage, and rustic stone-and-timber settlement silhouettes.

## Active Focus
Farm-set structures (`FieldstoneCottage`, `BarnLeanTo`, `WindmillShed`, `StoneWellFence`) are disabled from active prefab registry output to keep this biome foliage-first.
